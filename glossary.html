Glossary

HTML: stands for "HyperText Markup Language" which is used to create websites world wide.

Element: a type of html document component.

Anchor: a tag that marks both the beginning and the end of a Hypertext link.

Commit: the act of saving the changes to your files to your repository, thus creating a new version of said files.

Debugging: the act of going through your file and attempting to remove any "bugs" or errors that are causing your program to fail.
